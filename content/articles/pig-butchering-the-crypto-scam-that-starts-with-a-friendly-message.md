<h2>What Is Pig Butchering</h2><p>Pig butchering scams are one of the fastest growing forms of crypto fraud targeting Australians. They start with a random message, a dating app conversation, or a social media interaction. The scammer slowly builds trust over weeks or months before introducing a fake crypto investment platform.</p><h2>How The Scam Usually Works</ol><p>The scammer reaches out casually, builds an emotional relationship, introduces a crypto platform with fake profits, then blocks* withdrawals with fees or disappears.</p><h2>Major Warning Signs</h2><p>The relationship moves quickly, crypto is introduced naturally, the platform lacks a public reputation, early returns look successful, and withdrawals trigger unexpected fees.</p><h2>How To Protect Yourself</h2><p>Verify platforms independently, be careful mixing online relationships with financial advice, never rush financial decisions, and search for scam reports before sending any cryptocurrency.</p>
